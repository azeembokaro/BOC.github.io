The subject of this paper is on:

1. What is the foundation, inherent in humans, responsible for creativity?

2. Why only few could able to access / stimulate it?

And, the paper justify with an evidence to demonstrate that it indeed works to solve hard challenges – herewith provided a new design of Secure and Trustworthy Cryptographic Code based Electronic Voting System with the below features:

1. Vote Secrecy - voter's choice is anonymous, also voter can't prove to others and

2. Vote Verifiability - system ensures voter’s vote cast is immutable and voter is sufficiently convinced that his/her vote cast is taken into counting

These two features are polar opposite in nature, the more anonymous/secret the vote is, it is less verifiable and vice-versa. It fulfil these two features, using a quantum like classical technique, which are very hard to achieve and doesn’t exist in any of the known EVM technologies in the world.
